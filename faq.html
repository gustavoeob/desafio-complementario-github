Frequently asked questions

Can I pay with two different payment methods?

Yes. you can combine payment methods, you can make payments through our platform with both local and international Visa® and MasterCard® Cards and we also accept bank transfer. We don't accept cash.

How can I get free delivery?

Add and buy products that make $35 or more (not including taxes) in single order and to the same address to get free delivery.

Do I receive my products in 24 hours If I'm a prime member?

Yes. you are elegible to receive your purchased products in 24 hours only on working days.

I'm not happy with my purchase, can I return it?

If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can bring it back to a Mountain.co store of your choice within the first 10 days.
